Read it as two families, not one trend. The harnesses that encode knowledge — conventions, skills, review habits — supply what a weaker model lacks, and their value grew (KB 0 → +15.25, kprojects +0.5 → +7.75, both at or below control cost). The harnesses that demand the model drive an autonomous process need capability it doesn't have, so the process becomes overhead it must service (Phoenix −3 → −12, gstack −8 → −9.25). Actionable version: the weaker your model, the more you want conventions and the less you want ceremony.
